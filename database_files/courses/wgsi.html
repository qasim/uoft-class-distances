Women and Gender Studies [WGS courses]

The following timetable information is the most current information available. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

For all 'PE' indicator courses (400-level courses): During the first round of ROSI enrolment, certain enrolment restrictions apply to 400-level Women and Gender Studies courses. Enrolment restrictions vary from course to course. Eligible students must have completed 2.5 full course equivalents in Women and Gender studies. Students in their first or second year of study (with 8.5 credits or less) are not permitted to enroll in 400-level courses. Specialists and Majors in Women and Gender Studies in their 3rd and 4th year of study have access to certain courses during the first round of ROSI enrolment. For details, please see the courses below. During the second round of ROSI enrolment, enrolment is only through the department and students must complete a ballot form. Ballot forms are available on-line at www.wgsi.utoronto.ca/undergraduate/undergraduate-forms. Ballot forms must be submitted to the Undergraduate Administrator at the Women and Gender Studies Institute Program Office and are subject to the approval of the Course Instructor and/or Undergraduate Coordinator.

For all 'E' indicator courses (400-level courses): Please contact the Undergraduate Administrator, Women and Gender Studies Institute Program Office, Room 2036, Wilson Hall, New College, 40 Willcocks Street.

Latest Timetable Updates: Please visit our website www.wgsi.utoronto.ca/undergraduate/courses.
