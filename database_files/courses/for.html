The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2012-2013 Registration Handbook. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

For FOR301H1: This course is a ten day field camp held in Ontario. Students should contact the Faculty by May 18, 2012.

For JFG470H1 (administered by Geography): see GGR course listings.

For 'E' indicator courses: application forms are available for download and submission from the Forestry website or contact Amalia Veneziano at the Faculty Office, room 1016E, Earth Science Centre, 33 Willcocks Street, from April 6. Apply to the Faculty of Forestry by Friday, August 17. Late applications may be considered on an individual basis. For Faculty of Forestry information see www.forestry.utoronto.ca.
