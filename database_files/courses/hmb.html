The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2012-2013 Registration Handbook. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

Due to our rapidly expanding course menu, please check the online timetable and our website (hmb.utoronto.ca) for course updates.

For 'E' and 'PE' indicator courses: contact the Human Biology Program Office Room 105, Wetmore Hall, New College.

For 'AE' indicator courses, please refer to the Enrolment Indicator description in the Registration Handbook and Timetable. If your initial enrolment was not successful, please contact the Human Biology Program Office to waitlist for the course, Room 105, Wetmore Hall, New College.
