Cell and Systems Biology [CSB courses - includes BIO130H1, 230H1, 255H1, 260H1, 270H1 and 271H1]

The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2012-2013 Registration Handbook. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

For BIO130H1: Practicals (laboratories) meet in alternate weeks (A): practical meeting section numbers that end with 1 start the week of January 14; practical meeting section numbers that end with 2 start the week of January 21. Laboratory enrolment can be done on the SWS until the evening of Thursday, January 3. Late lab enrolment or lab changes must be done in the BIO130H1/230H1/BIO255H1 Office (ES 3053) starting Monday, January 7 until January 11.

For BIO230H1: Practicals (laboratories) meet in alternate weeks (A):PRACTICAL meeting section numbers that end with 1 start the week of September 17; PRACTICAL meeting section numbers that end with 2 start the week of September 24. Check portal for more information. Laboratory enrolment can be done on the SWS until the evening of Thursday, September 6. Late lab enrolment or lab changes must be done in the BIO130H1/230H1/BIO255H1 Office (ES 3053) starting September 10 until September 14. For your BIO230H1 laboratory classes, if you plan to take any other course laboratory on the same day in alternating weeks, check the start dates.

For BIO255H1: Practicals (laboratories) meet every week and start Friday, September 14. Check portal for more information. Laboratory enrolment can be done on the SWS until the evening of Thursday, September 6. Late lab enrolment or lab changes must be done in the BIO130H1/230H1/BIO255H1 Office (ES 3053) starting September 10 until September 13. Check portal and http://www.csb.utoronto.ca/undergraduate/courses/200 for more information.

For BIO260H1: Tutorial enrolment can be done on the SWS until the evening of Sunday, January 6. Late tutorial enrolment or tutorial changes must be done in the BIO260H1 Office (RW 424E) after this date.

For BIO270H1: Laboratory enrolment can be done on the SWS until the evening of Sunday, September 9. Late lab enrolment or lab changes must be done in the BIO270H1/271H1 Office (RW 424E) after this date.

For BIO271H1: Laboratory enrolment can be done on the SWS until the evening of Sunday, January 6. Late lab enrolment or lab changes must be done in the BIO270H1/271H1 Office (RW 424E) after this date.

For CSB325H1: Students must register for one of the tutorial sessions which meet every third week.

For CSB349H1: enrolment in a tutorial section is done on the SWS until Thursday, September 6. Late tutorial enrolment or changes must be done in the CSB349H1 Office (ES 3053).

For CSB352H1: The lecture for all students is on Thursdays at 4:00 and will be approximately 30 minutes in length. The lectures will be recorded so that students in the Friday lab who may not be able to attend the Thursday lecture; can watch the lecture before their lab on Blackboard. All students will be doing the same labs for the same length of time on Thursdays and Fridays in spite of the discrepancy in the number of hours listed.

For CSB397Y0: For students going on a 3rd-year exchange program through CIE and are interested in doing a research course while abroad, contact the CSB Undergraduate Office (416-978-8879).

For CSB445H1, 474H1, 483H1 and 491H1: students must submit an application form to the CSB Undergraduate Office (RW 424) by Friday, June 22. Forms are available at www.csb.utoronto.ca/undergraduate/forms.

For CSB497H1 F, 498Y1 and 499Y1: students must submit a completed CSB 497H1/498Y1/499Y1 contract to the Undergraduate Office (RW 424) by Wednesday, September 19, before being registered. Contracts are available at www.csb.utoronto.ca/undergraduate/forms.

For CSB497H1 S: students must submit a completed CSB497H1 contract to the Undergraduate Office (RW 424) by Wednesday, January 16, before being registered. Contracts are available at www.csb.utoronto.ca/undergraduate/forms.

For PE indicator courses: after the priority period has ended, obtain approval in the Cell and Systems Biology Undergraduate Office (RW 424). Forms are available at www.csb.utoronto.ca/undergraduate/forms.

Departmental Hours: Monday to Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. and 2:00 to 5:00 p.m.
