Anthropology [ANT courses - includes ARH]

The following timetable information is the most current information available. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

For ANT100Y1: there are mandatory tutorials. You will receive instructions outlining when and how to enroll in tutorials. Enrolment for tutorials will be through Blackboard.

Most 200 level ANT courses and some 300 level courses have tutorials/practicums. Please register through SWS for the tutorials/practicums, as applicable. There will be NO tutorials during the first week of classes.

For E indicator courses: see the Undergraduate Coordinator, Anthropology Building, 19 Russell Street, Room 258, Monday to Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. and 2:00 to 5:00 p.m.
