Employment Relations (IRE Courses)
IRE courses are available to students enrolled in the Employment Relations subject post (St. George Campus). It is not possible to accommodate non-program/non-degree students in these courses. Prerequisites are enforced.

IRE446H1: This seminar will include a compulsory learning format component using a service learning placement with a community-based agency or organization where students contribute a number of hours per week (usually 2 to 3 hours) to an agency or organization that she/he has been matched to.
