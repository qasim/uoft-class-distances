Biochemistry [BCH courses - includes BCB]

The following timetable information is the most current information available. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

For E indicator courses: contact the Undergraduate Administrator, Department of Biochemistry, 1 King’s College Circle, Room 5207.

Every course in the Department of Biochemistry has either prerequisites or exclusions. Please do not enroll in any Biochemistry courses until you have consulted the course description in the Arts and Science Calendar. Students will be removed from the course if they do not have the proper prerequisites or if they have completed an exclusion course. For students who are not in one of the listed indicator programs (consult the Arts and Science Registration Handbook & timetable) and wish to take a Biochemistry course, please contact Jennifer Haughton; email jennifer.haughton@utoronto.ca or phone 416-978-2700. You must email your request to Ms. Haughton requesting enrolment, and include your full name, student number and the course in which you wish to enroll. You request will be reviewed and you will be contacted via email with the result.

For PE indicator courses: eligible students must enroll via the SWS, students that are not eligible must email with Student ID to Jennifer Haughton; jennifer.haughton@utoronto.ca

For AE indicator courses: students must email, with Student ID to Jennifer Haughton; jennifer.haughton@utoronto.ca

For E indicator courses: students must email, with Student ID to Jennifer Haughton; jennifer.haughton@utoronto.ca

Office Hours: Monday to Friday, 9:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. and 2:30 to 4:00 p.m.
