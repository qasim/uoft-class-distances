Rotman Commerce [RSM courses - includes MGT201H1]

The following timetable information is the most current information available. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

RSM (Rotman School of Management) courses are restricted to students in Rotman Commerce and other approved programs in the fall/winter sessions. All students are responsible for fulfilling prerequisites and co-requisites; students enrolled in courses for which they do not have the published prerequisites will have their registration in those courses cancelled at any time without warning. Students must also observe exclusions. Failure to meet these requirements may result in academic difficulties. If students withdraw from a course they must also withdraw from any course for which it is a co-requisites.

Rotman Commerce students are given priority in enrolling in RSM courses according to the Faculty of Arts & Science enrolment dates for degree students. St. George Campus students in an approved program other than Rotman Commerce (see below) will be eligible to enroll in 200+ level RSM courses as required for their programs (pending class space) starting on Tuesday July 29, 2014 at 4:00 p.m. Note: enrollment in MGT 201H1S will start on Tuesday July 15, 2014.

Approved programs other than Rotman Commerce in the Fall/Winter sessions include: Actuarial Science Specialists*; Engineering Science students in the Engineering mathematics, Statistics and Finance option; Woodsworth Employment Relations students; Woodsworth Human Resource Management students. *Major in Actuarial Science students may take MGT 201H as well.

Consult the Rotman Commerce website at www.utoronto.ca/rotmancommerce/timetable for RSM course timetable information.
