The following timetable information is the most current information available. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

For BIO130H1S: Practicals (laboratories) meet in alternate weeks (A): practical meeting section numbers that end with 1 start the week of January 12; practical meeting section numbers that end with 2 start the week of January 19. Laboratory enrolment can be done on the SWS until the evening of Sunday, January 4. Late lab enrolment or lab changes must be done in the BIO130H1/230H1/BIO255H1 Office (ES 3053) starting January 5 until January 9.

For BIO230H1F: Practicals (laboratories) meet in alternate weeks (A): practical meeting section numbers that end with 1 start the week of September 15; practical meeting section numbers that end with 2 start the week of September 22. Check portal for more information. Laboratory enrolment can be done on the SWS until the evening of Sunday, September 7. Late lab enrolment or lab changes must be done in the BIO130H1/230H1/BIO255H1 Office (ES 3053) starting September 8 until September 12. For your BIO230H1 laboratory classes, if you plan to take any other course laboratory on the same day in alternating weeks, check the start dates.

For BIO255H1F: Practicals (laboratories) meet every week and start Friday, September 12. Check portal for more information. Laboratory enrolment can be done on the SWS until the evening of Sunday, September 7. Late lab enrolment or lab changes must be done in the BIO130H1/230H1/BIO255H1 Office (ES 3053) starting September 8 until September 11. Check portal and http://www.csb.utoronto.ca/undergraduate/courses/200 for more information.

For BIO260H1S: Tutorial enrolment can be done on the SWS until the evening of Sunday January 4. Late tutorial enrolment or tutorial changes must be done in the BIO260H1 Office (RW 424E) after this date.

For BIO270H1F: Laboratory enrolment can be done on the SWS until the evening of Sunday September 7. Late lab enrolment or lab changes must be done in the BIO270H1/271H1 Office (RW424E) after this date.

For BIO271H1S: Laboratory enrolment can be done on the SWS until the evening of Sunday January 4. Late lab enrolment or lab changes must be done in the BIO270H1/271H1 Office (RW424E) after this date.

For CSB325H1F: Students must register for one of the tutorial sessions which meet every third week. Tutorial enrolment can be done on the SWS until the evening of Wednesday September 10. Late tutorial enrolment or changes must be done in the CSB325 Office (RW424E) after this date.

For CSB330H1S: after the priority period has ended and if you did not have priority for this course, obtain approval in the Cell and Systems Biology Undergraduate Office (RW 424). Forms are available at www.csb.utoronto.ca/undergraduate/forms.

For CSB349H1S: enrolment in a tutorial section is done on the SWS until Sunday January 4. Late tutorial enrolment or changes must be done in the CSB349H1 Office (ES 3053) from January 5 to January 9.

For CSB397Y0Y: For students going on a 3rd year exchange program through CIE, who are interested in doing a research course while abroad, contact the CSB Undergraduate Office (416-978-8879).

For CSB430H1S: after the priority period has ended and if you did not have priority for this course, obtain approval in the Cell and Systems Biology Undergraduate Office (RW424). Forms are available at www.csb.utoronto.ca/undergraduate/forms

For CSB445H1F: students must submit an application form to the CSB Undergraduate Office (RW424) by Friday June 20. Forms are available at www.csb.utoronto.ca/undergraduate/forms

For CSB474H1S: students must submit an application form to the CSB Undergraduate Office (RW424) by Friday June 20. Forms are available at www.csb.utoronto.ca/undergraduate/forms

For CSB497H1F, CSB498Y1Y, CSB499Y1Y: students must submit a completed CSB497H1F/CSB498Y1Y/CSB499Y1Y contract to the Cell and Systems Biology Undergraduate Office (RW424) by Friday September 12, before being enrolled. If this is the only course you are taking in 2014-15, the contract must be submitted by Monday August 18. Contracts are available at www.csb.utoronto.ca/undergraduate/forms

For CSB497H1S: students must submit a completed CSB497H1 contract to the Cell and Systems Biology Undergraduate Office (RW424) by Wednesday January 14, before being enrolled. If this is the only course you are taking in 2014-15, the contract must be submitted by Monday August 18. Contracts are available at www.csb.utoronto.ca/undergraduate/forms
