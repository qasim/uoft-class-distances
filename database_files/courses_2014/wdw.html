Woodsworth ONE:
WDW151H1 and WDW152H1 lectures 0101, 0201 and 0301 are restricted to students in their first year of undergraduate studies (St. George campus) who have been admitted to Woodsworth One. WDW151H1 and WDW152H1 lecture 2001 is restricted to students in the full-time Academic Bridging Program.
