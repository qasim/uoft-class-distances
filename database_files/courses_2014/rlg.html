The following timetable information is the most current information available. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

Prerequisites: Students without proper prerequisites may be removed from the course. Please see the Arts & Science calendar for prerequisite requirements.

For RLG100Y1 and 280Y1: Tutorials are included in the lecture time, for L0101 Friday 12:00 to 1:00 p.m. and for L5101 Thursday 5:00 to 6:00 p.m. Students will be informed about their tutorial groups during the first two weeks of class. There will be no tutorials during these two weeks.

For RLG210Y1/SOC250Y1: Tutorials will be held Wednesday 5:00 p.m. The Instructor will announce tutorial dates in class. There will be no tutorial the first week of class.

For E indicator courses: please see information on how to enroll in this type of course in the undergraduate section of the Religion website at http://www.religion.utoronto.ca/programs/undergraduate/.
