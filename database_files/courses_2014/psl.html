For E indicator courses: Contact Eva Eng, 1 King’s College Circle, Medical Sciences Bldg., Room 3209, Monday to Thursday 8:30 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.

For AE indicator courses: Contact Eva Eng, 1 King’s College Circle, Medical Sciences Bldg., Room 3209, Monday to Thursday 8:30 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
The selection of students into AE Indicator courses takes place by August 2. Before August 2, status is interim, on August 2, the status changes to approved or refused for successful or unsuccessful applicants respectively. Students requesting AE courses are advised to enroll in an alternative course until the status of their request is confirmed. There will be a departmental waitlist on the Physiology website for AE courses after July 29, 2014.


For PSL498Y1/499H1, 'E' indicator courses: students are required to find a supervisor in the Department of Physiology. Please visit our website for ballot and course information at: http://www.physiology.utoronto.ca/undergrad/courses/psl498y.htm.
