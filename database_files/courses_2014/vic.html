The following timetable information is the most current information available. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

For Vic One Hundred courses, 101H1, 104H1, 106H1, 107H1, 112Y1, 117H1, 119H1, 135H1, 159H1.
'RP' Indicator courses: restricted to 1st Year Arts & Science Students (Victoria College). On August 8 priority exists for 1st Year Arts & Science Students (St. George Campus). These courses are for first year students only.

For VIC One courses, 150Y1, 151Y1,162H1, 163H1, 164H1, 165H1, 170Y1, 171Y1, 172Y, 173Y1, 181H1, 183H1, 184H, 185H1, 190Y1, 191Y1. 'E' Indicator courses: these courses are only open to students accepted into the Vic One program. For questions, please contact vic.one@utoronto.ca.

For JSV200H1, JSV201H1, JSV202H1. 'E' Indicator course: these courses are only open to CTEP and Education and Society students. For questions, please contact vic.ctep@utoronto.ca.

For VIC360H1/Y1, VIC362H1, VIC363H1. 'E' Indicator course: these courses are only open to Education and Society students.

Application courses (please note the different due dates):

For VIC275H1, 280H1, 479Y1, 480H1, 'E' Indicator course: students must submit an application by June 1st. Visit www.vic.utoronto.ca/students/academics/creative/courses for the application.

For VIC401H1. ‘E’ Indicator Course: students must submit an application for entry into the course. Visit www.vic.utoronto.ca/students/academics/lct/courses for the application.

For VIC476H1: ‘E’ Indicator course: students must submit an application by June 1st. Visit www.vic.utoronto.ca/students/academics/capstone for the application.

For VIC493H1. ‘E’ Indicator course: students must submit an application by April 30.Visit www.vic.utoronto.ca/students/academics/capstone for the application.

For all VIC independent study courses, please submit an application (check website for each term’s deadlines): www.vic.utoronto.ca/students/academics/independentstudies

For all other 'E' indicator courses: students must follow procedures and deadlines as listed in the Calendar.

Unless otherwise indicated, please contact vic.academics@utoronto.ca for further information.
