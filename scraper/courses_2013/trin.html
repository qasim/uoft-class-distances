The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2012-2013 Registration Handbook. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

For TRN190Y1: This course is restricted to students in their first year of undergraduate studies, St. George campus. Places in the course are reserved for Trinity students during the priority period in the first enrolment round but are open to students from any college on the St. George campus when priorities are removed on August 9.

For 'E' indicator courses: contact H. Joel, Room 300, Larkin Building, Monday to Friday from 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. .

Departmental Hours (September 4 to 7): Monday to Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.
