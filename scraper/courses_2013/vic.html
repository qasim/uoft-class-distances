Victoria College Courses [VIC courses - includes JSV200H1]

The following timetable information is the most current information available. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

For JSV200H1, 201H1 and 202H1, 'E' indicator course: this course is only open to SMC CTEP & VIC Education and Society Minors (CTEP and Arts Program) students. For questions contact vic.ctep@utoronto.ca.

For Vic One Hundred courses (formerly known as Pathways), 101H1, 104H1, 105H1, 106H1, 109H1, 112Y1, 113H1, 119H1, 135H1. 'RP' indicator courses: restricted to 1st Year Arts & Science Students (Victoria College). On August 8 priority exists for 1st Year Arts & Science Students (St. George Campus). These courses are for first year students only.

For Vic One courses, 150Y1, 151Y1,162H1, 163H1, 164H1, 165H1, 170Y1, 171Y1, 172Y1, 173Y1, 181H1, 183H1, 184H1 and 185H1, 190Y1, 191Y1. 'E' indicator courses: these courses are only open to students accepted into the Vic One program. For questions please contact vic.one@utoronto.ca.

For VIC209H1: R indicator course: this course is restricted to upper year students, and is a 6 week condensed course starting in mid-October.

For VIC223Y1 P indicator course: this course is an introduction to Semiotics. Priority given to students enrolled in the Semiotics Program (Minor, Major, Specialist) and 1st and 2nd Year Arts & Science Students (St. George Campus).

For VIC224Y1 RP indicator course: Restricted to upper year students from St. George Campus, priority to Material Culture Minor.

For VIC275H1, VIC350Y1 'E' indicator courses: students must submit an application by June 1. Visit the Victoria College Website www.vic.utoronto.ca for more information, or contact vic.academics@utoronto.ca.

For VIC322H1, 324H1, 'P' indicator courses: Priority given to students registered in the Semiotics program (Minor, Major, Specialist).

For VIC401H1 E indicator Courses: Students must contact the instructor for permission, and then have the instructor email their consent to vic.academics@utoronto.ca

For all other 'E' indicator courses: students must follow procedures and deadlines as listed in the Calendar. Application material should be submitted to vic.academics@utoronto.ca.
