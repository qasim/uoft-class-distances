Classics [CLA courses - includes GRK, and LAT]

The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2012-2013 Registration Handbook. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

CLA 300-level seminars, P indicator: Priority is given to students enrolled in the named programs after the first request period. Students should check that they are already in the correct program before applying. Pre-requisites will be enforced. Students requesting a waiver of pre-requisites should do so before attempting to enroll; contact, undergrad.classics@utoronto.ca.

For E indicator courses (Independent Study): Applicants should first contact a potential supervisor for the proposed project. Once they have the supervisors agreement, they should bring it to the Undergraduate Coordinator, Room 110, 125 Queens Park or email undergrad.classics@utoronto.ca.
