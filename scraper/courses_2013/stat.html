The following timetable information is the most current information available. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

Tutorials begin the second week of classes. Locations will be posted on Blackboard by the instructor or TA. If the students need to change a tutorial, please use the SWS through September.

For 'AE' indicator courses: students should apply directly into the course by July 28; after this date, contact the instructor to receive approval, Department of Statistics 100 St. George St, Room 6018.

For 'E' indicator courses: students should obtain application forms from the Department of Statistics, Sidney Smith Hall, 100 St. George Street, Room 6022.
