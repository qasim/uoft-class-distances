Physiology [PSL courses]

The following timetable information is the most current information available since the publication of the 2012-2013 Registration Handbook. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

For PSL299Y1 'E' indicator: please see http://www.artsci.utoronto.ca/current/undergraduate/course/rop/299#intro.

For 'AE' indicator courses: The selection of students into AE Indicator courses takes place by August 3. Before August 3, status is interim, on August 3 the status changes to approved or refused for successful or unsuccessful applicants respectively. Students requesting AE courses are advised to enroll in an alternative course until the status of their request is confirmed. There will be a departmental waitlist on the Physiology website for AE courses after July 25, 2012.

For PSL498Y1/499H1, 'E' indicator courses: students must obtain applications at MS 3209, and submit them by August 24, 2012.

Departmental Hours (September 4 to 7): Monday to Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m.
