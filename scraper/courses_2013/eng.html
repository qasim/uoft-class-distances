English [ENG courses - includes JEI206H1]

The following timetable information is the most current information available. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

Students will be removed from courses if they fail to note the prerequisites and exclusions that apply. 400-level courses: During the priority period, July 15 to August 6, fourth-year Specialists are allowed to enrol in only 1.0 400-level ENG FCE and fourth-year Majors who require a 400-level ENG course to complete their 2010 or later program are allowed to register in only 0.5 400-level ENG FCE. Students in other programs or years of study may register in 400-level ENG courses after the priority period ends. Exceeding these limits will result in removal from the extra courses during the priority period.

For ENG140Y1: students are to enroll in a tutorial section, F12 or F1.

For ENG150Y1: students are to enroll in a tutorial section, F10 or F11.

For ENG201Y1: students enrolled in L0101 ONLY are to enroll in a tutorial section, F9 or F10.

For ENG201Y1: students enrolled in L0201 ONLY are to enroll in a tutorial section, R3 or R4.

For ENG202Y1: students are to enroll in a tutorial section, R10 or R11.

For ENG220Y1: students enrolled in L0201 ONLY are to enroll in a tutorial section, R3 or R4.

For ENG252Y1: students enrolled in L0101 ONLY are to enroll in a tutorial section, W10 or W11.

For ENG287H1: students are to enroll in a tutorial section, W5 or W6.

Please note that a $135 ancillary fee will be charged to students enrolling in ENG434H1F L0101.

For 'E' indicator courses: students must visit www.english.utoronto.ca for information, and then submit an application or signed proposal form by May 15 to V. Holmes, Department of English, 10:00 a.m. to 12:00 and 2:00 to 4:00 p.m.

PLEASE NOTE THAT OUR COURSES REACH CAPACITY QUICKLY.
