Peace, Conflict and Justice Studies [PCJ courses]

The following timetable information is the most current information available. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

For further information: contact the Program Advisor: Lucinda Li, lucinda.li@utoronto.ca or 416-946-8450.

Students not in the PCJ program must have approval from the Program Administrator to take PCJ courses.

For PCJ260Y1, PCJ360H1, PCJ361H1, PCJ460H1 and PCJ461H1, 'PE' indicator courses: as of August 15, Departmental approval is required. Please contact the pcj.programme@utoronto.ca.

For 'E' indicator courses: students should email the program administrator for approval, pcj.programme@utoronto.ca.

For further information, contact the Trudeau Centre for Peace and Conflict Studies Program Office, pcj.programme@utoronto.ca.
