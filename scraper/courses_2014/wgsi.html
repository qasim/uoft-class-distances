Women and Gender Studies [WGS courses]

The following timetable information is the most current information available. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

For all “RP” indicator courses (400-level courses): During the first and second round of ROSI enrolment, certain enrolment restrictions apply to 400-Series Women and Gender Studies courses. During the “R (restricted)” round, 3rd and 4th-year Specialists and Majors in Women and Gender Studies are eligible to enrol. During the “P (priority)” round, priority is given to third and fourth-year Specialists, Majors and Minors in Women and Gender Studies. Students from outside Women and Gender Studies are also eligible to enrol during the P round. Eligible students must have completed 2.5 full course equivalents in in Women and Gender Studies. Students in their 1st or 2nd year of study with 8.5 credits or less are not permitted to enrol in 400-Series courses.

For all “RE” indicator courses (400-level courses): This enrolment indicator only applies to WGS460Y: Honours Seminar. During the “R (restricted)” round of enrolment, only 4th-year Majors and Specialists in Women and Gender Studies are eligible to apply. During the “E” round of enrolment, all students must ballot at the department. For details, please contact the Women and Gender Studies Institute Program Office, Room 2036, Wilson Hall, New College, 40 Willcocks Street. Email: gradwomenstudies@utoronto.ca

For all “E” indicator courses (400-level courses): Students must ballot at the department. Please contact the Women and Gender Studies Institute Program Office, Room 2036, Wilson Hall, New College, 40 Willcocks Street. Email: grad.womenstudies@utoronto.ca.

Latest Timetable Updates: Please visit our website: http://www.wgsi.utoronto.ca/undergraduate/courses.
