Laboratory Medicine and Pathobiology [LMP courses]

The following timetable information is the most current information available. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

For LMP365H1, LMP406H1 and LMP415H1 'PE' indicator courses: Non-Pathobiology Specialists may be considered for enrolment. If space allows, students with similar backgrounds may be given special permission to enroll. For further information and/or instructions, contact the LMP Undergraduate Office, Medical Sciences Bldg (MSB), room 6209. (Earliest consideration for enrolment starts in June until August).


For LMP405Y1 (Project in Laboratory Medicine & Pathobiology): contact the LMP Undergraduate Office, Medical Science Bldg, room 6209 or visit the departmental website http://www.lmp.facmed.utoronto.ca/undergrad/artsc/lmp405y.htm


For PE indicator courses: contact the LMP Undergraduate Office prior to August 7, Medical Science Bldg (MSB), room 6209.

For E indicator courses: contact the LMP Undergraduate Office prior to the end of August, Medical Science Bldg (MSB), room 6209.
