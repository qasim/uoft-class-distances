Diaspora and Transnational Studies [DTS courses]

The following timetable information is the most current information available. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

For program inquiries, please contact Antonela Arhin, Executive Officer, via email, cdts@utoronto.ca or phone 416-946-8464.

For student counseling, please contact Ken MacDoanld via email, cdts.undergraduate@utoronto.ca.

For 'PE' indicator courses: During the priority period, July 15 to August 13, students who are in the DTS grogram will be given priority in terms of enrolling in one of the DTS400-level courses. During open enrolment, August 15 to September 20, 'PE' course enrolment is administered by the Centres Executive Officer, Antonela Arhin. Students who need to take one of the DTS 400-level courses to graduate in 2013-2014 are urged to enroll early. Kindly send an email to Antonela Arhin at cdts@utoronto.ca providing the following information: your name, student number, whether you are in the DTS major or minor program, and the course you want to enroll in. The deadline for submitting this information is September 20, 2013 for 'F' courses and January 17, 2014 for 'S' courses.

For 'E' indicator courses (Independent Study): Applicants should first contact a potential supervisor for the proposed project. Bear in mind that the topic needs to have bearing on diaspora and transnationalism. Any member of the U of T Faculty can supervise the applicant. Contact Antonela Arhin at cdts@utoronto.ca to inquire about a form that needs to be filled out. The deadline for submitting the form is September 20, 2013 for 'F' and 'Y' courses, and January 17, 2014 for S courses.
