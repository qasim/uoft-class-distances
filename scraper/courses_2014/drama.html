Centre for Drama, Theatre and Performace Studies [DRM courses - includes JDC410H1 and JIA400H1]

The following timetable information is the most current information available. Courses and meeting sections added will be listed below. Courses and meeting sections cancelled will be indicated as such.

For DRM200Y1: Admission is based on an audition and an interview, held in mid-April. For those students who cannot attend April auditions, another set of auditions is held in August. Students must apply online in March or early August.

For DRM228H1: Admission is based on a portfolio submission in May.

For DRM254Y1: Admission is based on an interview in April. Students must apply online in March or early August.

For DRM328H1 and DRM486H1: Admission is based on a portfolio submission due in October.

ForDRM328H1, DRM302H1, DRM402H1, DRM403Y1 and DRM486H1: these courses also include mandatory tutorials for rehearsal purposes which will be held regularly and scheduled in the first week of class each term. Dates and times to be determined based on student availability.

For DRM354Y1: Admission is based on an interview in April. Students must apply online in March.
Please note: Performance classes (DRM200Y1, 300Y1 and 400Y1) require a Voice co-requisite (DRM201H1, 301H, 401H) and a Movement co-requisite (DRM211H1, 311H1, 411H1). The section a student is placed in for a Performance class must be the same section for their Voice and Movement co-requisites. Both Voice and Movement classes include mandatory tutorials which will be held up to 3 times each term per student and scheduled in the first day of class.

For E indicator courses: For all practical courses, students need to register for an application online to schedule their audition and/or interview. Contact the Undergraduate Coordinator, Centre for Drama, Theatre and Performance Studies (Drama Centre), 79 St. George Street, Room 302 or 416-978-8099. Applications are due in March and can be obtained from the departmental office or from the website: http://dramacentre.utoronto.ca/students/login.php

For the latest updates and additional information on the Drama Centre, consult our web page http://dramacentre.utoronto.ca/
